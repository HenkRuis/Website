From a rough sketch to a locked forme, every job passes through five hands.

  1. Compose

    Type is set letter by letter from the case, or a plate is cut for larger imagery.

  2. Lock up

    The forme is wedged tight in the chase so nothing shifts under pressure.

  3. Ink

    Oil-based ink is rolled by hand until the coverage is even across every letter.

  4. Press

    Each sheet is pulled through the iron press one at a time, by foot treadle.

  5. Finish

    Trimming, folding, and — where it's a book — sewing the signature by hand.
